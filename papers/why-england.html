Why England? Demographic Factors, Structural Change and Physical Capital Accumulation during the Industrial Revolution

Nico Voigtländer, Hans-Joachim Voth

Journal of Economic Growth 11, pp. 319-361 (2006)

Published version Code

Abstract

Why did England industrialize first? And why was Europe ahead of the rest of the world? We present a probabilistic two-sector model where the initial escape from Malthusian constraints depends on the demographic regime.
